Data Transfer

The final and most important stage of joining our platform is to establish what the most effective and practical way is for you to obtain the customer information from us. We offer two highly secure processes that you can choose from, to ensure that when we transfer you the data it is protected as much as possible. These variations are listed below:

  • Batch files
  • API Push

Batch files by SFTP

Procedure

  • High level of security and automation
  • Loaded between 0300 and 0400 everyday
  • Loaded into either FTP, S3 or SFTP

Example: e.g. ELX_SupplierName_20130729040101_001_V1.8.csv

Features

  • Most Popular method
  • All switches up to a few seconds are included
  • Frequency and timing of load can be customised
  • Files are only sent when they exist (unless discussed)

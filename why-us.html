Why us

It has been a constant battle for small to medium sized businesses to source an energy supplier who fulfils their exact needs. Businesses have become more conscious of their overheads and are looking to money saving sites to get a personalised deal. So, we have come up with an efficient way to connect you to your customers, by providing sufficient information to make an informed decision of how they could benefit from switching to you. This allows you as the supplier to enhance recognition in the market, receive a better reputation and gain new customers switching to your services every day from a variety of different areas all over the UK. As a result, you will be following regulations by allowing your pricing to be transparent within the market.

By using our platform, you will be utilising a free sales channel to a wider audience and gain an increase of tariff applications on top of your direct sales channel. Since being one of the leading energy software businesses in the SME market we have a direct online process for you to present and receive applications, allowing you to be in complete control of what type of businesses can take a tariff, the terms and conditions they need to follow and much more. Then once we receive an application it is immediately sent off to you for approval.
